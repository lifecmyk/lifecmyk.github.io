a guarantee of success for any event

The brand PARADIS begins its history in 1967, dedicated to the catering of big events, for more than 50 years. In Europe it has successfully carried out more than 90,000 events, collaborates with more than 23 michelin stars, and throughout Europe it was the first company to obtain the certificate of sustainable catering of sosevents, awarded by the EU. It is the first company that applies the trazacalidad concept to the circle of the kitchen garden with excellence in Europe.

To date it has more than 200 restoration projects, which include, among other restaurants, hotel F & B projects, cafeterias, F & B projects for fairs and central kitchens. Distinguished in the catering sector in Spain, its geographic dispersion of catering activities extends not only throughout the country, it is also punctually in Portugal, France, Andorra, Switzerland, Germany, Holland, New York (The first authentic food restaurant Spanish in 1990), Czech Republic, Mexico and China.

Since 1967

Mr. Armando

Mr. Armando begins his training in architecture. Life takes him by accident to take the reins of what will be his first restaurant, recovering a manorial estate and turning it into the current Can Amat complex. From that moment, he will always remain linked to the world of restoration.

Forerunner in his time in this sector, he develops a novel concept in the creation of teams, totally opposed to the traditional "weekend bolo". At the same time, it starts up the Central Kitchen, starting in Spain and later in the rest of the world.
