Mr. Armando begins his training in architecture. Life takes him by accident to take the reins of what will be his first restaurant, recovering a manorial estate and turning it into the current Can Amat complex. From that moment, he will always remain linked to the world of restoration.

La Morana

En La Morana, queremos hacer un pequeño esfuerzo por esta forma de vida respetando los ciclos de la tierra y la naturaleza, llegando a las personas con productos vegetales y animales en las mejores condiciones de envejecimiento, conservación y sabor. Esta es nuestra razón de ser, y nuestra forma humilde de devolver a la naturaleza todo lo que nos brinda.

In La Morana we want to do our bit to this way of living respecting the cycles of the earth and nature, reaching people with plant and animal products in the best conditions of aging, conservation and flavor. This is our reason for being, and our humble way of giving back to nature all that it gives us.

在La Morana 蔬果园，我们想以这样的生活方式来致敬地球与大自然的生态圈：将蔬果与肉品以最好的熟成状态、最新鲜的保存方式及最棒的味觉口感送达人们的餐桌。 这就是我们存在的理由，以及我们以这样一种谦恭的方式回馈大自然。
